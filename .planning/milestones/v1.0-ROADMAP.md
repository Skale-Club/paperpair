## Summary

First shippable version of PaperPair: a guided immigration document assistant for couples filing marriage-based Adjustment of Status (AOS). The applicant can now complete the full journey from eligibility screener through interview prep — with DB-persistent timeline, AI chat with history, document upload and extraction, income calculator, and submission packet assembly.

## Key Accomplishments

1. **Database persistence layer** — Migrated timeline progress, chat history, forms selection, document metadata, and generated PDFs from localStorage/memory/disk to Prisma + Supabase Postgres + Supabase Storage. No data loss on Vercel cold starts.
2. **Security hardening** — AI legal guardrails (refuses eligibility/legal questions), UPL disclaimer across chat/forms/screener, USCIS edition locks on all 6 form packs, MIME magic byte validation for uploads, rate limiter upgrade.
3. **Full case flow end-to-end** — Eligibility screener with entry-type filtering and EWI warnings, forms browser with plain-English instructions + USCIS links, AI chat that extracts names/dates/addresses conversationally into the case profile, private per-user document upload with AI auto-extraction to form fields.
4. **Completion tools** — I-864 income calculator using 2026 HHS poverty guidelines with 125% threshold and joint sponsor explanation, submission packet page with Advance Parole travel warning, form download list, assembly checklist, and USCIS lockbox link.
5. **Interview prep** — 30-question USCIS interview bank across 4 categories with flashcard UI, 8-item interactive what-to-bring checklist, role-differentiated tips with petitioner/beneficiary self-select toggle.
6. **Auth + spouse invites** — Email/password + Google OAuth, session persistence across refreshes, petitioner-to-spouse invite flow with shared case access via `viewerOfId` linking.
